My Educational Portfolio

My recent projects in the field of education, just "click" on them to learn more.

STEAM school "RoSTEM"

My partners and I have opened a school at the university where school subjects are taught in the STEM education paradigm — that is, not just studying biology, physics and mathematics as part of the curriculum, but combining them into a new "whole". Disciplines are taught in terms of communication with each other. This allows us to consider and solve problems more comprehensively and globally, and not in parts, relying only on one area. Children from 7 to 18 years old study at our school in the following areas: programming, robotics, electrical and circuit engineering, 3D printing and 3D modeling.

Educational project "Growing with RoSTEM"

Together with the administration of Rostov-on-Don and the Ministry of Education of the region, he conducted more than 30 webinars for schoolchildren of grades 7-11, on various topics in the STEM education paradigm. Each of the webinars was attended by more than 20 schools, which is equivalent to an audience of ~ 1000 people.

The cycle of educational STEAM courses

A series of online educational courses with kits that receive delivery. For children from 7 years old, I quietly explain in practice the school curriculum in mathematics, physics and computer science, as well as the basics of programming and electrical engineering. Getting hard and soft-skills with our courses will make it easier for a child to study at school, he will start using the phone not only for YouTube and games. Link to the first course of the series.

MATH & RStudio online courses for students

An educational platform where my colleague Maria and I teach R and help students enroll in European Universities. We have developed training courses in mathematics and R-studio for undergraduate students. The courses will help you to understand all the topics in mathematics and R included in the curriculum for the 1st, 2nd and 3rd semesters of study.

What our students get: learning becomes easier, there will be no incomprehensible questions; all tests and exams are passed; only the necessary information for solving practical problems and understanding the material; a set of standard tasks and answers to test questions on the platform; Life Webinars, where we analyze with teachers all the tasks that caused difficulties.
